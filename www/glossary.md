# Glossary

**AQS (EPA Air Quality System)**: EPA system of record for air monitoring data; includes annual summaries. Not real-time.

**PLACES (CDC)**: Model-based (MRP) local estimates of health outcomes and risk factors, derived from BRFSS and other inputs.

**SVI (CDC/ATSDR Social Vulnerability Index)**: Relative percentile rankings of vulnerability; not directly comparable across years.

**CBI (Compound Burden Index)**: Transparent composite score combining standardized pollution, health burden, and vulnerability.
